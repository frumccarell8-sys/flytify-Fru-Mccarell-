About the Anime

Demon Slayer follows the journey of Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose family is attacked by demons. After discovering that his sister Nezuko has been transformed into a demon, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps to seek a cure and avenge his family.

Main Characters

Tanjiro Kamado

The determined protagonist who fights demons using Water Breathing techniques.

Nezuko Kamado

Tanjiro's sister who became a demon but still protects humans.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

A fearful but incredibly powerful swordsman who uses Thunder Breathing.

Inosuke Hashibira

A wild fighter known for his Beast Breathing style and boar mask.
